Located right in front of the pyramids on the Giza plateau, this bed and breakfast features a rooftop terrace with barbecue facilities. There is a shared kitchen, and free herbal tea and coffee are provided. A free airport transfer is available upon arrival.

Pyramids view offers air-conditioned rooms and apartments with free Wi-Fi. The rooms are simply furnished and feature satellite TV and a private bathroom. The apartments offer a spacious living room. Horse riding and camel excursions can be arranged. After a busy day, guests can watch the sound and light show of the pyramids from the rooftop. There is a mini-market, a bakery and a pharmacy within a 5-minute walk from pyramids View Inn, and Cairo International Airport is a 25-minute drive away. A free airport shuttle is available upon request.
